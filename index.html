Get to know Team Buttons - the gorgeous people behind the patterns, who work hard to bring our business dreams to life, helping you learn to sew your own clothes!

DIRECTOR

TILLY WALNES

Tilly runs the company, overseeing strategy and creative direction. She is the author of three sewing books, Love at First Stitch, Stretch! and Make It Simple, and writes and presents instructions for our patterns, workshops and blog. Tilly has been named 'Most Inspirational Sewing Personality' in the British Sewing Awards and appeared on the first series of BBC2’s The Great British Sewing Bee in 2013. She has been writing about sewing since 2010 and has nearly 20 years’ experience of designing courses and learning resources. She is a Clore Fellow, and previously worked for the Independent Cinema Office, British Film Institute and V&A.

She is a mum to two little boys who keep her very busy when she's not working. Oh and she’s addicted to stripes.
